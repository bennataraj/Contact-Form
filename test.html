About Us

The key to any business relationship is to make sure the goals of everyone involved are met. When it comes to fundraising for Political groups, PACs, and Non-Profit organizations, Right Field Consulting Group specializes in helping its partners not just meet but exceed those goals through effective acquisition, maximizing the net income raised, and implementing a plan to make sure your members and donors always have a positive experience when interacting with your group.

Right Field Consulting can help with every aspect of your telephone fundraising including developing the initial strategy, creative, training, and fulfillment, as RFCG has experience with:

  • 501(c)3 and 501(c)4 Non-Profit Groups
  • Political Candidates
  • Political Action Committees
  • 527 Groups
  • Inbound Product Sales/Service
  • Direct Response Television

Matt Birkbeck

President

Matt began his career in the Direct Marketing Industry in 2000 as an Account Representative with InfoCision Management Corporation in Akron, Ohio. Over the next 15 years, as an Account Executive and later, Director of Political Marketing, he was privileged to work with a diverse collection of clients that included Political Action Committees, Non-Profit Organizations, and Candidates for Federal and State Office. Through these different programs and positions, Matt gained experience in a variety of skills including new member/donor acquisition, fundraising, creative development, pledge fulfillment, member/customer service, and overall development strategy.

A graduate of Orrville High School in Orrville, Ohio, Matt earned a Bachelor of Science in Education with a double minor in History and Political Science from Bowling Green State University. He is married with two daughters.
